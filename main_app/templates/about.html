What is Fish Buddy?

Fish Buddy is a solution to a problem that myself and my fellow fishing buddies were having with remembering our previous catches and what we used to catch our fish. This app allows you to have a personalized page where you can log the species that you catch, what body of water you caught it at, what gear you used to catch it, and as a little plus, the ability to track you catches by length, weight, etc...
